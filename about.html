Owner profile

Priya Sharma, Makeup Artist & Salon Specialist

Priya founded Lumina Beauty Studio after years of working with brides, models, and everyday clients who wanted beauty services that felt personal, hygienic, and premium. Her work blends soft glam, modern Indian bridal detailing, skin-first preparation, and polished hair styling.

She focuses on listening first: understanding the client’s outfit, skin type, comfort level, event timing, and photography needs before building the final look.
